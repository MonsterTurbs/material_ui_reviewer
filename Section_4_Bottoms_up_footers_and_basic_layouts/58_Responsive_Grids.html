🙈 Hiding Footer Links Responsively

🧩 What is the Hidden Component?

The Hidden component from Material UI lets you show or hide anything based on screen size (breakpoints). Perfect for making responsive sites without writing custom CSS media queries!

📥 Step 1: Import Hidden

At the top of Footer.js, add:

import Hidden from '@material-ui/core/Hidden';

🔀 Step 2: Wrap Your Grid

Wrap the entire footer grid in a <Hidden mdDown>…</Hidden> tag to hide it on medium (md) screens and smaller:

<Hidden mdDown>
  <Grid container spacing={2}>
    … your link columns …
  </Grid>
</Hidden>

🔍 mdDown Explained

  • mdDown hides content on medium and small screens (and below).
  • Links appear on large screens but disappear on tablets & phones.

📱 See It in Action

1. Save and refresh your app.
2. Resize the browser: links show on wide screens, vanish on narrow ones.
3. Drawer navigation still works on mobile!

💡 Why Use Hidden?

  • ✅ Keeps mobile footer clean & simple.
  • ✅ Uses existing breakpoint system—no extra CSS needed.
  • ✅ Great for toggling any UI piece by device size.
