1. Page Container 🗂️

Wrap the whole page in a <Grid container direction="column"> so each section stacks top-to-bottom. 😊

2. Title & Mission ✨

• First <Grid item> holds <Typography variant="h2">About Us</Typography> (left-aligned title).
• Next <Grid item container justify="center"> with <Typography variant="body1" align="center" style={{ fontStyle: 'italic' }}> for the mission statement. Center it on the page, but the text itself is centered too. 🎯

3. History Section 📜

• Create a row: <Grid item container direction="row">.
• Left side: <Grid item container direction="column"> with:
  - <Typography variant="h4">History</Typography>
  - Multiple <Typography variant="body1" paragraph> paragraphs (copy your history text).
• Right side: <Grid item><img src={historyIcon} alt="history icon"/></Grid>. 🖼️

4. Team Section 🏆

• Use <Grid item container direction="column" alignItems="center"> to center content.
• Add your name/title with <Typography variant="h4">, then a bio line with body1.
• Insert <Avatar src={yourPhoto} alt="Your Name" /> for a nice round image (e.g., your photo or dog pic). 🐶

5. Fun Facts Icons 🎉

• Under your bio, add a row: <Grid item container direction="row" justify="center">.
• For each fun fact (3 total):
  - Use <Grid item container direction="column">.
  - First child: <img src={icon} alt="icon description"/>.
  - Second child: <Typography variant="caption">Caption text</Typography>.
• Repeat for all three icons. 📊📷💡

6. Review & Export ✅

Save your code, preview, and check alignment at different screen sizes. Congrats—you’ve mastered a complex About Us layout! 🚀
