1. Billing Policy Change 🔄

Firebase now requires the Blaze plan to deploy Cloud Functions, meaning you need a payment method on file. 📋

2. Blaze Plan Benefits 🔥

The Blaze plan includes all free usage from the Spark plan, so you won't incur charges unless you exceed free limits. 👍

3. Add a Payment Method 💳

To attach billing:

1. Go to Firebase console.
2. Click the gear icon next to “Project Overview”.
3. Select “Usage and billing”.
4. Open the “Details & settings” tab.
5. Add your payment method.

4. Why Billing Is Required ❓

  • Prevents accidental overuse of resources.
  • Ensures you have a way to pay if you exceed free quotas.
  • Google handles everything securely—no hidden fees for basic course usage.

5. No Course Charges 📚

For our course demo usage, you stay within free limits. You only need billing on file; you won’t be billed unless you go over. 😊

6. Questions? 🤔

If you run into any issues setting up billing, let us know. We're here to help and hope this change isn't inconvenient!
