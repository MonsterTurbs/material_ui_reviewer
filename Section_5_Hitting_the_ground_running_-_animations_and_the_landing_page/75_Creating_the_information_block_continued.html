🖼️ Add Emoji with Accessibility

  • Wrap the emoji in a <span>:
  • <span role="img" aria-label="waving hand">👋</span>
  • This makes sure screen readers describe it as “waving hand”.

⚙️ Fix Positioning Overlap

  • Both items had position: absolute and overlapped.
  • Wrap both grid items in one container.
  • Move position: absolute onto that container instead.

↔️ Space & Alignment

  • About Us keeps margin-left; Contact Us uses margin-right.
  • Add the small (sm) prop on both grid items → makes them auto width.
  • Set Contact Us text-align: right so it aligns within its half.

⬇️ Responsive Stacking

  • Import useMediaQuery and check matchesXS for extra-small screens.
  • On the wrapping container, set direction={matchesXS ? "column" : "row"}.
  • Center text when stacked: textAlign={matchesXS ? "center" : undefined}.

🔀 Margins & Spacing for Small Screens

  • Reduce side margins at small sizes: margin (sm) ? "2rem" : "5rem".
  • Use nested ternary for Contact Us to remove margin on extra-small: matchesXS ? 0 : (matchesSM ? "2rem" : "5rem")
  • Add spacing={matchesXS ? 10 : 0} on the container to separate stacked items.

✅ Result

  • On large screens, About Us & Contact Us each take 50% width and align left/right.
  • On the smallest screens, they stack vertically, centered, with proper spacing.
  • Your layout is now responsive and user-friendly! 🎉
