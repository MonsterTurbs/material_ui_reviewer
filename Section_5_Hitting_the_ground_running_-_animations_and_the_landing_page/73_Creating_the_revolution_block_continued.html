📘 Study Notes: Revolution Block

⚡ Add Space Above the Block

We set a margin-top of 12m on the Revolution container so it doesn’t sit too close to the previous section. This keeps things neat!

✨ Style the Title

Our title uses the H3 Typography variant with:

  • Font: Pacifico ✍️
  • Size: 2.5rem
  • Color: dark blue

Then we add gutterBottom to put a little gap under the title for breathing room.

📐 Responsive Padding

On small screens, big padding can push our card off the sides. So we use a breakpoint to change padding:

  • padding-top & padding-bottom: 8m
  • padding-left & padding-right: 0

🎯 Flush Corners on Mobile

When squeezed on small screens, let’s remove the rounded corners and stretch full width:

  • border-radius: 0
  • width: 100%

👍 Final Result

The Revolution card now looks polished, floats nicely, and adapts perfectly on all devices. Great job! 🎉
