🎥 Easy Animations with Bodymovin

✨ Why Animations?

Animations add fun and professionalism to your website. The first thing visitors see is your Hero animation! 🌟

🔍 What is Bodymovin?

Bodymovin is a free After Effects plugin that exports animations as JSON and images so you can play them in React apps. 📜➡️⚛️

📥 Step 1: Download Bodymovin

  • Go to aescripts.com/bodymovin.
  • Click “Name Your Price”, enter $0 if you like, and sign up or log in. 🆓
  • No payment info needed—just place your order for $0.

💾 Step 2: Get ZXP Installer

Download the ZXP installer at aescripts.com/learn/zxp-installer. This helps you install the plugin into After Effects. 🛠️

📂 Step 3: Install Bodymovin

  • Unzip Bodymovin zip file you downloaded.
  • Open ZXP Installer app and drag the Bodymovin .zxp file into it.
  • Enter your admin password when prompted. ✅

🎬 Step 4: Open in After Effects

  • Launch After Effects.
  • Go to Window > Extensions > Bodymovin.
  • Bodymovin panel shows all your compositions to export.

🚀 Step 5: Export Your Animation

  • Select the composition you want.
  • Click the folder icon (…) to choose a save location (e.g., Desktop).
  • Click Render to export JSON and image assets.

🔧 Next Steps

Move your exported JSON and images into your React project. We’ll tweak the files slightly before using a Lottie library to play animation. 🎉
