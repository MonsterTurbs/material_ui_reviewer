🤖 What is Robots.txt?

It's a simple text file that tells search engines where they can and can't go on your website. 🚦

📜 What is Sitemap.xml?

A map for search engines that shows how your website pages are connected and when they were last updated. 🗺️

🔍 Why are Robots.txt and Sitemap Important?

  • 🛡️ Robots.txt protects your site by controlling what search engines can see.
  • 📅 Sitemap.xml helps search engines know when you've updated your pages.
  • 🚀 Both improve your site's visibility and performance on search engines.

📝 Example Robots.txt

User-agent: *
Sitemap: https://yourwebsite.com/sitemap.xml
Disallow:

"User-agent: *" means "all search engines welcome!" 🌟

📅 Example Sitemap.xml

<urlset>
  <url>
    <loc>https://yourwebsite.com/page</loc>
    <lastmod>2024-04-27</lastmod>
  </url>
</urlset>

"lastmod" shows when the page was last updated. 📆

⚙️ Automating with Scripts

Automatically update Robots.txt and Sitemap.xml whenever you change your website! 🤖🔄

✅ Quick Recap

  • 🤖 Robots.txt = rules for search engines.
  • 🗺️ Sitemap.xml = map of your site.
  • 🔧 Use automated scripts to make it easy!
