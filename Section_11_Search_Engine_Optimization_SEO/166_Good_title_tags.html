📘What is a Title Tag?

Every HTML page has a <title> element in its head that tells browsers and search engines what the page is about.

It appears in browser tabs and search engine listings (the clickable headline).

❓Why Good Titles Matter

1. Search Results: Your title shows up as the main link in Google or Bing (SERP).

2. Browser Tabs: Users see this text in their tabs—helpful for easy navigation.

3. SEO Importance: Title tags are one of the top on-page ranking factors after page content.

✔️3 Rules for Great Titles

Short & Sweet: Keep ~60 characters. Too long & it gets cut off…

Include Core Keywords: Use the main topic (e.g. "Custom Software Estimate").

Be Compelling: Give users a reason to click (e.g. "Free", "Top", "Fast").
