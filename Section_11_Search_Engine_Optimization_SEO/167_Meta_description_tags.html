🌟 What is a Meta Description?

🔍 A meta description is a short paragraph you see under the title on search results (like Google). It tells you quickly what a webpage is about.

📘 Example of Meta Description

Facebook example: "Create an account or log in to Facebook. Connect with friends, family, and other people you know."

❓ Why is it Important?

  • 🖱️ Helps people decide if your website has what they're looking for.
  • ✨ Makes words bold when they match the search keywords, attracting clicks.
  • 📢 Acts like a mini-advertisement for your website!

🎯 What Makes a Good Meta Description?

  • 🔢 Around 160 characters long.
  • 🔑 Includes important keywords.
  • 🌈 Unique and specific—not boring!
  • 📣 Has a clear call to action (e.g., "Sign up now!" or "Get a free quote!").

👍 Example of a Great Meta Description

"We provide the fastest, modern, and affordable software design services. Get a free estimate now!"

✅ Contains keywords (software design)

✅ Gives value (fastest, affordable)

✅ Has a call to action (get a free estimate)

📲 Social Media Use

When you share your webpage on social media, it also uses the meta description as the little preview text!

🛠️ How to Add Meta Description in Code

Put this inside the HTML <head> section:

<meta name="description" content="Your webpage description goes here.">

📈 Does it Affect Google Ranking?

No, Google says meta descriptions don't directly affect ranking. But they do help people decide if they want to click on your link! 🖱️✨

🎨 Quick Recap

  • 📝 Short and interesting
  • 🎯 Has keywords
  • 📢 Encourages action
