🚀 What are Open Graph Tags?

They help your webpage look nice when shared on social media! 📱

📖 Who Created Open Graph?

Originally made by Facebook to help links look good when shared. 👍

🌍 Why Are Open Graph Tags Important?

  • 🔗 Makes links attractive and clickable.
  • 📸 Adds pictures, titles, and descriptions to link previews.
  • 🎉 Used by Facebook, Twitter, iMessage, and more!

🎨 Example of Open Graph

When you share a website link on social media, it shows:

  • 🖼️ Image preview
  • 📌 Title and short description

⚙️ Setting Up Open Graph Tags

Add these tags inside your webpage's <head>:

<meta property="og:type" content="website">
<meta property="og:title" content="Your Awesome Title">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://yourwebsite.com/page">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://linktoyourimage.com/image.png">
<meta property="og:image:type" content="image/png">
<meta property="og:image:width" content="1200">
<meta property="og:image:height" content="630">
<meta property="og:image:alt" content="Your Image Description">

🖼️ Image Tips

  • Recommended size: 1200 x 630 pixels 🎯
  • Use public image URLs (like imgur.com) 🌐
  • Add descriptive "alt" text 📖

💡 Quick Recap

  • 🔍 Helps attract clicks on social media.
  • ✅ Doesn't directly change your ranking, but improves engagement.
  • ✨ Easy to set up and makes your links look professional!
