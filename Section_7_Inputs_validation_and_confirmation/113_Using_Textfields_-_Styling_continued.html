1. The Centering Problem 🤔

We tried alignItems="center" on a full-width container, but it wouldn’t move because the grid item already spans the entire width. 🚫

2. Wrap Your Content 📦

Cut out the inner Contact Us section and wrap it in its own: 

<Grid item>
  <Grid container direction="column">
    …your Contact Us content…
  </Grid>
</Grid>

This lets you center that wrapper without it already filling 100%. 👍

3. Center the Send Button 📨

Turn the button’s wrapper into a container and add: 

<Grid item container justify="center">
  <Button variant="contained">Send Message</Button>
</Grid>

Your button now truly sits in the middle! 🎯

4. Adjust Column Sizes 📐

To give more breathing room on large screens, change: 

<Grid item xl={3} lg={4}>…</Grid>
<Grid item xl={9} lg={8}>…</Grid>

This keeps a 3/9 split on extra-large, and a 4/8 split on large. ✅

5. Add Background Padding 🏞️

To push the background content up a bit, in your classes.background style: 

paddingBottom: '10rem'

6. Tweak Text Margin ↔️

Reduce the left margin on your “Simple Software…” text: 

marginLeft: '3rem'

Now it sits nicely closer to the edge. 😉

7. Add Spacing Around Contact Info ⏫⏬

  • Phone container: marginTop: '2rem'
  • Email container: marginBottom: '2rem'
  • Send button container: marginTop: '2rem'

8. Ready for Responsive Design 📱💻

Now everything lines up nicely. Next, we'll add breakpoints to adjust layout on tablets and phones!
