🚀 What is Deployment?

Deployment means publishing your website so everyone can visit it at a URL instead of just on your computer. 🌐

⚙️ Why Deployment Matters

  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Share with friends and family.
  • 🔗 Get a real web address (like mycoolapp.com).
  • 🔎 Let search engines find your site.

😌 Easier Than Ever!

In the past, deploying React apps was tricky. Now, many services automate it in just a few clicks! ✨

🛠️ Option 1: Heroku

  • 🗄️ Popular platform for many projects.
  • 📦 Easy to connect your code and deploy.
  • 🎓 Great for beginners and experienced devs alike.

🌐 Option 2: Vercel

  • 🚀 Made by the creators of Next.js.
  • 🔧 Super simple setup with powerful free features.
  • 🔄 Automatic previews and fast builds.

🔍 Deploying Twice to Compare

We’ll deploy the same project on both Heroku and Vercel to see how each works and feels. 🆚

🔗 Adding a Custom Domain

After deployment, you can connect your own domain (like www.yourname.com) so your site looks professional! 🎉

✅ Quick Recap

  • 🚀 Deployment makes your site live for everyone.
  • ⚙️ Use services like Heroku or Vercel for easy setup.
  • 🔍 Compare both by deploying twice.
  • 🌐 Add a custom domain for a neat web address.
