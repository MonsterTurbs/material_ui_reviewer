🚀 What is Now (Vercel)?

Now (now called Vercel) is a super-easy hosting platform made by the creators of Next.js. It offers:

  • 🔧 Zero-config setup—just push your code!
  • 🔒 Free SSL certificates for secure HTTPS.
  • 🌍 Automatic global CDN for super-fast loading everywhere.

👍 Why Use Vercel?

  • 📦 Perfectly built for Next.js apps.
  • ⚡ Instant deployments with a single command.
  • 🔄 Easy GitHub integration for automatic deploys.

✍️ Step 1: Sign Up for Free

  • 1️⃣ Go to vercel.com and click “Sign Up.”
  • 2️⃣ Enter your email and confirm via the link they send you.
  • 3️⃣ Choose a username and complete your profile.

💻 Step 2: Install & Log In via CLI

Open your terminal and run:

npm install -g now
now login

Enter your email when prompted and confirm in your inbox. ✔️

🚢 Step 3: Deploy Your App

In your project folder, just run:

now

That’s it—Vercel will build and give you a live URL! 🎉

🔍 Step 4: Verify & Test

  • 📋 Copy your deployment URL and open it in a browser.
  • ✉️ Test forms and interactive features to make sure everything works. 🛠️

🌐 Dashboard & Domains

In your Vercel dashboard, click your project to see:

  • 🔗 Live deployments history.
  • 🗂️ Domains tab to add custom URLs.

🔑 Step 5: Add a Custom Domain

In the Domains tab:

  • 1️⃣ Click “Add” and enter your domain (e.g., myapp.com).
  • 2️⃣ You’ll see required nameservers (e.g., ns1.vercel-dns.com).
  • 3️⃣ In your domain provider, update your nameservers accordingly.
  • 4️⃣ Wait for DNS to propagate (up to 48 hours).

✅ Quick Recap

  • 🚀 Sign up on Vercel and install now CLI.
  • 📂 Run now in your project folder.
  • 🔗 Test your live URL and features.
  • 🌐 Add a custom domain and update nameservers.
  • 🎉 Enjoy fast, secure hosting with free SSL & CDN!
