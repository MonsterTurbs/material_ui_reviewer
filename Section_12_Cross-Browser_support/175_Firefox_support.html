🔥 Why Test in Firefox?

  • 🎯 Firefox is a popular browser with significant web traffic.
  • ✅ Ensures your site looks and works great for all users.
  • 🔄 Many fixes for Safari also solve Firefox issues.

🔎 Testing Process

  • 📐 Start full-screen on Home page, scroll and check visuals.
  • ↔️ Resize to just above medium breakpoint, re-check layout.
  • ↕️ Enter medium breakpoint, verify element behavior.
  • ⬇️ Shrink to just above small breakpoint, inspect again.
  • 📱 Enter small breakpoint, confirm responsive design.
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Optionally test even smaller widths for edge cases.

🖥️ Page-by-Page Steps

Repeat the above process on each page:

  • 🏠 Home
  • 📋 Services
  • 💻 Custom Software Development
  • 📱 iOS & Android App Development
  • 🚀 Revolution
  • ℹ️ About Us
  • ✉️ Contact Us
  • 📊 Estimate

✔️ Functionality Checks

  • 📧 Submit the Contact form—verify emails arrive.
  • 🧮 Use the Estimate tool—choose options & request estimate.
  • 🔄 Ensure interactive elements respond correctly.

📐 Responsive Boundaries

  • ⚖️ Test at each breakpoint boundary (just above/below).
  • ❌ Watch for layout glitches or misaligned elements.
  • ✅ Confirm consistent spacing, fonts, and icons.

🔄 Consistency Across Browsers

  • 🔧 Safari fixes often carry over to Firefox.
  • 🔁 After changes, re-test in Chrome to avoid regressions.
  • 🌐 Aim for a unified experience everywhere.

✅ Quick Recap

  • 🖥️ Full to small—thoroughly test visuals at all sizes.
  • 🛠️ Validate both look and functionality (forms, tools).
  • 🔍 Catch and fix issues before users spot them.
